About.

SEREGA PIRAT - Streamer, Dota 2 player, musician, video blogger and voice actor. He became known mainly for his funny voiceovers of films and also for some tracks performed as parodies. Nowadays, he conducts live broadcasts on the Twitch platform, playing various computer games, including Dota 2. Currently he lives in Vietnam where he still has a studio in which he broadcasts and records music, collaborating with other artists.
